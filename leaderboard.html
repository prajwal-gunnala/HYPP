For NGOs:

Rules and Guidelines

Impact: Every child who completes a HYPP program module earns 1 point for your NGO. Bonus points for exceeding program goals or demonstrating innovative implementation.
Collaboration: Partner with other NGOs for joint HYPP initiatives - earn 1 point per participating NGO.
Sharing: Actively share success stories and best practices on the HYPP platform - 1 point per impactful story.
Community Engagement: Conduct outreach events like parent workshops or community awareness campaigns - 1 point per event.
Sustainability: Develop long-term plans for continued youth development - 1 point for each sustainable project adopted.

For Students::

Participation: Active engagement in all HYPP program modules - 1 point per module completed.
Leadership: Taking initiative and demonstrating leadership qualities within the program - 1 point per instance.
Academic Improvement: Showing noticeable improvement in grades or skill-based assessments - 1 point per improvement.
Community Service: Volunteering to support their community or younger students - 1 point per hour volunteered.
Creative Expression: Sharing original work (art, writing, etc.) inspired by the HYPP program - 1 point per unique submission.

Bonus Points:
Overcoming Challenges: Students demonstrating exceptional resilience or overcoming personal obstacles - bonus points awarded by program mentors.
Innovation: Submitting creative ideas or solutions to improve the HYPP program - bonus points awarded by program judges.
Mentorship: Students who help and support their peers within the program - bonus points awarded by peers.
