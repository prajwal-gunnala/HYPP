About Us
Step into the world of HYPP—Harnessing Youth Potential Program
—where education knows no bounds. A sanctuary for deprived
children, a platform for integrating NGOs, fundraisers, and students
globally to impart knowledge and shape futures.
We’re the Robin Hoods of education, stealing knowledge from the
cities and distributing it in remote villages. As in an adrenaline-
charged relay race, students turned teachers pass the baton of
knowledge to the village children, changing lives one lesson at a
time.
It’s not about charity, but about promoting a fair chance at success.
Those who do well—be it NGOs, student-teachers or protegés—
earn points for their efforts. Partnering companies then boost these
accumulated points, providing job advantages and additional
funding. A continuous cycle of giving, learning, and prospering.
Associating with HYPP has broadened our
horizons on impacting the lives of
marginalized children

Questions You Might Have
How to be a part of HYPP?
Join us as an NGO, fundraiser, or student and be the
change you want to see.
Who can teach in this program?
Students willing to make a difference are welcome from
all colleges and schools to teach.
What’s in it for the student-teachers?
You may not get paid but you get something far more
valuable – experience, accommodation, and food. Plus,
valuable points to give an edge in your career!
Can these points be redeemed?
Absolutely! NGOs and students can use these points as
a leverage for job advantages and funding benefits.
