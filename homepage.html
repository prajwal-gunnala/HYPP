barriers.

How It Works?
Who are we?
We are a digital hub that connects
NGOs, fundraisers, underprivileged
children, and students worldwide.
Through our platform, we establish an
effective education vigilance system in
rural areas and small villages where
education support is limited.
What do we do?
We provide vital activities to NGOs,
allowing them to reach colleges and
schools to hire students who are
willing to help by teaching young
minds from small villages. And
remember, it’s not just about giving,
there’s a lot to gain too!
What's in it for me?
For students, the opportunity to make
a difference, coupled with
accommodation, meals, and a unique
advantage in the job market. For
NGOs, the growth is more points-
based. The more you complete
activities, the more points you earn,
and the more funding you can receive
from fundraisers. Lastly, for the
aspiring students from the poor
sections, it’s a chance to break free of
their limitations and gain exposure to
a wealth of knowledge.
